SprtGuesser Game

About:

Welcome to The Summer SprtGuesser game!

We as a group have created this game for the 2019 summer hackathon.

How to play?

It's your job to guess the summer sport. Everytime you guess a wrong answer we will give you some help and unblur the image a little bit.

When you get it right you win! Then it's on to the next level and next sport!

Enjoy playing!
